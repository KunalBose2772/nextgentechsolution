## 5. Typical Discovery Call Agenda
Aim to spend 80% of the time listening and only 20% speaking. Follow this sequence:
1.  **Context Building:** *"Can you walk me through your current workflow and explain where you spend the most manual effort?"*
2.  **Identify the Pain Points:** *"What is the business cost of that bottleneck (e.g., lost leads, data errors, slow turnarounds)?"*
3.  **Define the Goal State:** *"What does a successful launch look like for your team in terms of speed, volume, or revenue?"*
4.  **Introduce the NextGen Solution:** Briefly present our capabilities or show a relevant case study/demo.
5.  **Next Steps:** Schedule a secondary scoping call with our Technical Solutions Architect to detail estimates and deliverables.
